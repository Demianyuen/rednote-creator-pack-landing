# Order the RedNote Creator Pack

Order page:

https://demianyuen.github.io/rednote-creator-pack-landing/#order

## Price

USD 12 per public RedNote / Xiaohongshu post pack.

## What to Send

- One public RedNote or Xiaohongshu URL
- Delivery email
- Preferred invoice method: PayPal invoice or Stripe invoice
- Optional notes about the goal for the post

## What You Receive

- Transcript notes when the source video audio can be processed
- OCR or visible post text when available
- Title, caption, hashtag, and keyword notes
- Hook ideas and caption drafts
- A concise repurposing brief

## Payment Flow

The order form submits an invoice request to support@xhsvideodownloader.com.

A PayPal or Stripe invoice is sent before processing starts. The finished pack is delivered by email after invoice payment.

## Processing Limits

Processing depends on whether the public post can be accessed and whether usable text, images, or audio are available.

If the post cannot be processed, you can send a replacement public URL or request a refund.
