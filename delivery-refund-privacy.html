RedNote Creator Pack policy notes

Delivery, refund, and privacy notes.

These notes explain what happens after you request the USD 12 RedNote / Xiaohongshu Creator Pack.

Delivery

The finished pack is delivered by email after invoice payment. The pack is structured as readable text and can include source URL, extracted visible text, transcript notes, hooks, captions, hashtags, and repurposing notes.

Delivery target is within 24 hours while the launch offer is open, but timing can vary if the public post is hard to access.

Refunds and failed posts

Processing depends on whether the public post can be accessed and whether usable text, images, or audio are available.

If the post cannot be processed, you can send a replacement public URL or request a refund.

Privacy

The order form asks for a public post URL, delivery email, preferred invoice method, and optional notes. These details are used to send the invoice and deliver the pack.

Do not send private account links, passwords, payment card details, or content you do not have permission to analyze.

Content rights

This service does not grant rights to reuse third-party content commercially. Use it only for content you have the right to analyze, archive, or repurpose.
