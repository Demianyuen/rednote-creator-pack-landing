# Sample RedNote Creator Pack

This is a sample structure for the USD 12 RedNote / Xiaohongshu Creator Pack.

## Source

- Platform: RedNote / Xiaohongshu
- Public URL: provided by customer
- Goal: turn one saved post into reusable creator notes

## Clean Transcript

The spoken content is rewritten into readable paragraphs when video audio can be processed.

Example:

> The post introduces a compact desk setup for creators who film short videos at home. The speaker highlights lighting, camera angle, and a simple background arrangement that keeps the setup clean without expensive equipment.

## Visible Text / OCR Notes

Text visible in images, captions, or screenshots is extracted when available.

Example:

- Home creator setup
- Three lighting positions
- Clean background
- Low budget filming

## Hook Ideas

- "A simple RedNote setup idea you can reuse today"
- "How this creator makes a small desk look studio-ready"
- "Three filming details hidden in this RedNote post"
